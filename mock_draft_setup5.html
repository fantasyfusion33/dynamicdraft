Welcome to the Fantasy Fusion Mock Draft!

Before your can start your mock draft, we need to let you know how the draft works and set up the details of your league.

How the Mock Draft Works:

    The Point of the Game:

    In Fantasy Fusion, instead of drafting players from one sport, you draft teams from across the 6 major US Sports (MLB, NBA, NFL, NHL, NCAAB, NCAAF). Each roster has 2 slots for teams from each sport to fill, with the potential to add additional Wildcard slots.

    The Draft Format:

    The draft is a standard snake draft format. In a snake draft, the draft order reverses each round, meaning the team that picks first in one round picks last in the next, creating a "snaking" pattern of selection.

    Mock Draft Details:

    On the next screen you will decide which pick you will have in the draft. You will have 2 minutes to make your selection. All other picks will be auto-drafted.

How to Set Up Your Mock Draft:

    1. Create Your League: Give your league a name and choose the number of teams in your mock draft.

    2. Set Your Draft Position: Find 'My Team' in the list below and use the arrow buttons to select your draft position.

    3. Add Wildcards (Optional): Choose the number of wildcard slots to include in your draft. Each slot adds an extra roster spot to draft an additional team from any sport.
